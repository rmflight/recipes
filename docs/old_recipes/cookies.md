# 3 Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

* 1 cup peanut butter
* 3/4 cup white sugar
* 1 egg

Mix together ingredients.
Roll into balls on a baking sheet.
Flatten with a fork.

Bake at 350F for 10 minutes.
